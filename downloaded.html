The Knowledge Keepers

**In a changing climate, this powerful documentary draws on some 65 thousand years of

Indigenous knowledge, to help us better prepare for the future.**

Stream free on SBS On-Demand

When a group of children gather round a campfire to hear stories

from First Nation practitioners about our rapidly changing climate, they are taken on a journey of discovery.

Traversing the country, we are introduced to storytellers who draw on the past to help us prepare for the future.

With thought provoking questions and answers, we learn about cultural practices for Fire, Flood, Heat, and weather preparedness.

"Knowledge is only powerful when it's shared."

Den Barber - Cultural Fire Practitioner

NRMA Insurance is proud to support the making of this film in partnership with Campfire x, an Indigenous creative-led agency. Our goal is to share Indigenous preparedness knowledge through storytelling.

Fire

Explore the benefits of healing country through controlled cultural burning and land management to reduce bushfire risk and maintain ecological balance.

Flood

Floods are a part of Australian life. Hear from traditional land practitioners hoping to build more disaster resilient communities.

Heat

From Indigenous architects to gardeners, discover different cooling strategies to reduce the impact of heat.
